
EXAMPLES
  clms add "polymarket api rate limit is 60/min" --tag infra
  clms add "we can arb this lag" --depends-on 1 --depends-on 3 --tag strategy
  clms add "mm bots withdraw at >50k size" --unverifiable --tag market
  clms add "ETH 1h-return distribution rejects uniform at p<0.01" --min-tier empirical

NOTES
  - --depends-on registers prerequisite claims. if any is later refuted with
    --cascade, this claim auto-flips to suspect.
  - --unverifiable is for claims you genuinely cannot test (subjective,
    future-dependent). honesty bucket. otherwise the claim starts pending.
  - claim text should be falsifiable. "x lags y by ~300ms" not "latency is bad".
  - --git-sha and --created-at are provenance overrides for archaeology /
    historical reconstruction. they require CLAIMS_BACKFILL=1 in the
    environment to use. claims born with either override are stamped
    `backfilled: true` on disk and rendered with [backfilled] in human
    output so a reviewer can distinguish live from retroactive provenance.
  - --min-tier stamps an evidence-tier floor on the claim at write time.
    valid: empirical | observed | documented | derived. when set, clms
    verify refuses to mark this claim Verified using evidence below the
    floor. use --min-tier empirical for science claims so a downstream
    agent cannot satisfy them with observed/documented evidence. claims
    without --min-tier behave exactly as before (no gate).
  - add is refused under CLAIMS_REPAIR=1 (repair mode is read-only).
